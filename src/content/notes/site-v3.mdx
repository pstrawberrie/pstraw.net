As of **August 5, 2025**, version 3 of pstraw.net is live and alive. As the second major design update to hit the site, this version tightens everything up and introduces a new unified theme with modern **spacing**, consistent **UI elements**, matching-ish **colors**, and more complementary **fonts**.

## V3 Changelog

### V3.0 - Aug 5, 2025

Added "chill space" theme

- Used [Bolt](https://bolt.new) to generate a cool space theme using [Star Trek: Prodigy](https://www.themoviedb.org/tv/106393-star-trek-prodigy) colors. It includes...
  - Increased spacing (waaaaay more space)
  - Cool space colors
    - <code class="border-primary">&nbsp;</code> primary
    - <code class="border-secondary">&nbsp;</code> secondary
    - <code class="border-tertiary">&nbsp;</code> tertiary
    - <code class="border-quaternary">&nbsp;</code> quaternary
    - <code class="border-quinary">&nbsp;</code> quinary
  - New nav layout
  - New card layouts & hover effects

### V3.1 - Aug 10, 2025
